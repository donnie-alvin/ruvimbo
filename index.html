About School

Ruvimbo Special School is a school for children with disabilities in Zimbabwe. It was founded in 1971 and currently has an enrollment of 101 children. The school caters for children with a variety of disabilities, including autism, Down syndrome, microcephaly, mental retardation, cerebral palsy, hyperactivity, and epilepsy.
The school provides a great learning environment for children with disabilities. The teaching staff is experienced and dedicated, and the school offers a variety of programs and services to meet the needs of its students. These programs include academic instruction, speech therapy, physical therapy, and occupational therapy.
Ruvimbo Special School is located in Southerton, Harare. The school is accessible by public transportation, and it has a bus that picks up and drops off students at designated stops.
The school is a valuable resource for children with disabilities in Zimbabwe. It provides them with a quality education and helps them to reach their full potential.
